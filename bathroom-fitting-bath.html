Bathroom Fitting in Bath

Expert bathroom installations for modern and period properties

Complete Bathroom Installation Services

Bath Plumbers provides professional bathroom fitting services across Bath and North East Somerset. Whether you are renovating a compact en-suite or transforming a spacious family bathroom, we manage every aspect from design consultation through to completion. Our experience with Bath's period properties means we understand how to create beautiful, functional bathrooms that complement your home's character.

We work with you to design a bathroom that meets your needs and budget. From contemporary minimalist designs to classic period-appropriate schemes, we bring your vision to life with quality materials and expert craftsmanship.

Our Bathroom Services

  • Complete bathroom renovations - Full strip-out and refitting
  • En-suite installations - Creating new en-suite facilities
  • Shower room conversions - Converting baths to walk-in showers
  • Period bathroom restorations - Sympathetic renovations for historic properties
  • Accessible bathrooms - Adaptations for mobility needs
  • Fixture replacements - Individual item installations

Period Property Expertise

Bath's Georgian and Victorian properties require specialist consideration for bathroom work. Issues such as solid walls, limited access for new pipework, and the need to maintain period character all inform our approach. We have extensive experience working with listed buildings and understand the requirements for listed building consent where applicable.

We can source traditional-style sanitary ware and fixtures that complement period architecture whilst incorporating modern functionality. Our plumbers are skilled at routing new pipework with minimal disruption to original features.

What We Include

  • Design consultation and planning
  • All plumbing work and connections
  • Removal and disposal of old bathroom
  • Coordination of tiling and electrical work
  • Installation of all sanitary ware
  • Quality silicone sealing and finishing

Areas We Serve

We install bathrooms throughout Bath including Bath City Centre, Widcombe, Oldfield Park, and all surrounding areas. Our coverage extends to Bradford-on-Avon, Keynsham, and across North East Somerset.
