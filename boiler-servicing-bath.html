Professional Boiler Servicing

Regular boiler servicing is essential for safety, efficiency, and maintaining your warranty. Bath Plumbers provides comprehensive boiler servicing across Bath and North East Somerset, carried out by Gas Safe registered engineers with experience across all major boiler brands.

An annual service ensures your boiler operates safely and efficiently, catching potential problems before they become expensive repairs. We recommend servicing your boiler before winter to ensure reliable heating when you need it most.

What Our Service Includes

  • Visual inspection of boiler and flue
  • Combustion analysis and efficiency check
  • Gas pressure and flow rate testing
  • Safety device inspection
  • Heat exchanger inspection
  • Condensate trap cleaning
  • Controls and thermostat check
  • Advice on any issues found

Boiler Repairs

If your boiler develops a fault, our engineers can diagnose and repair most problems quickly. We work with all major boiler brands including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, Ideal, and Potterton. Common repairs we carry out include:

  • Ignition and pilot light problems
  • Pressure loss and filling loop issues
  • Pump and motorised valve replacement
  • PCB and control failures
  • Heat exchanger repairs
  • Gas valve replacement

Landlord Gas Safety Certificates

Landlords are legally required to have gas appliances checked annually. We provide landlord gas safety certificates (CP12) for properties across Bath, ensuring you meet your legal obligations and keep your tenants safe.

Areas Covered

We provide boiler servicing throughout Bath and surrounding areas including Lansdown, Combe Down, Keynsham, Radstock, and across North East Somerset.
