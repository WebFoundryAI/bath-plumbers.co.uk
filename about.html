About Bath Plumbers

Your trusted local plumbing experts

Experienced Local Plumbers

Bath Plumbers has been serving the local community for many years, building a reputation for quality workmanship and reliable service. We are a team of experienced plumbers who take pride in our work and treat every property with care and respect.

Period Property Expertise

Working in Bath means working with some of the finest Georgian and Victorian architecture in the country. Our plumbers understand the unique challenges these buildings present, from original lead pipework to complex drainage systems. We work sensitively with listed buildings and understand conservation requirements.

Our Values

  • Quality - We complete every job to the highest standards
  • Honesty - Clear pricing with no hidden charges
  • Reliability - We arrive when we say we will
  • Respect - We treat your property as our own

Qualifications

All our plumbers are fully qualified and insured. Our gas engineers are Gas Safe registered, and we hold all relevant certifications for the work we undertake.

Service Area

We cover Bath, North East Somerset, and surrounding areas including Keynsham, Midsomer Norton, Radstock, and Bradford-on-Avon.
