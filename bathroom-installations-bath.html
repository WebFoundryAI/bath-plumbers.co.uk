Professional Bathroom Installation Services

Transform your bathroom with Bath Plumbers. We provide complete bathroom installation services across Bath and North East Somerset, from initial design through to the finishing touches. Whether you are renovating a bathroom in a Georgian townhouse or adding an en-suite to a modern property, our experienced team delivers beautiful, functional bathrooms that suit your property and lifestyle.

In a city famous for its Roman Baths, it seems fitting that we take bathroom design and installation seriously. We work with your ideas and requirements to create spaces that combine practicality with aesthetics, using quality materials and fixtures that will look great and last for years.

Our Bathroom Services

  • Full bathroom renovations - Complete strip-out and refit
  • En-suite bathrooms - Creating additional bathroom facilities
  • Wet room installations - Accessible, stylish shower rooms
  • Shower installations - Electric, mixer, and thermostatic showers
  • Bath replacements - Freestanding, built-in, and corner baths
  • Sanitary ware installation - Toilets, basins, and bidets
  • Bathroom repairs - Fixing leaks, replacing worn fixtures

Period Property Expertise

Installing bathrooms in Bath's historic properties requires particular care and expertise. Georgian and Victorian buildings often have solid stone walls, uneven floors, and limited space. We have extensive experience working in listed buildings and conservation areas, understanding how to install modern plumbing systems whilst respecting original architectural features.

We can advise on appropriate styles that complement period aesthetics, from traditional Victorian roll-top baths to discreet modern fixtures that do not detract from original character. We also navigate the practical challenges of routing pipework, ensuring adequate ventilation, and working within the constraints of older buildings.
