Rapid Emergency Plumbing Response

When a plumbing emergency strikes, Bath Plumbers provides rapid response across Bath and North East Somerset. Our experienced emergency plumbers are equipped to handle any urgent situation, from burst pipes flooding your home to blocked drains causing backup. We understand the distress that plumbing emergencies cause and work quickly to resolve the problem and minimise damage.

Bath's period properties present unique challenges during emergencies. Hidden pipework within thick walls, original lead supply pipes, and complex drainage systems require experienced hands. Our plumbers know these buildings and can respond effectively to protect both your property and its historic features.

Emergency Services We Provide

  • Burst pipes - Rapid isolation and repair to stop flooding
  • Severe leaks - Emergency leak location and repair
  • Blocked drains - Urgent clearance of blocked drains and toilets
  • Flooding - Response to internal flooding from any cause
  • No hot water - Urgent restoration of hot water supply
  • Boiler breakdowns - Emergency heating and hot water repairs
  • Overflowing tanks - Ballvalve and overflow repairs

What to Do in an Emergency

  1. Turn off the water - Locate your stopcock (usually under the kitchen sink or where the supply enters your property) and turn it off
  2. Call us - Phone 01225 123456 and describe what has happened
  3. Contain the water - Use towels, buckets, and containers to manage water while waiting
  4. Protect electrics - If water is near electrical fittings or appliances, switch off at the consumer unit
  5. Move valuables - If safe to do so, move furniture and valuables away from water
